Module 06: Cyber Defense

Become a Digital Guardian.

The internet is a vast ocean. To explore it, you need a strong shield. Learn to protect your data, your identity, and your village.

🛡️

Security vs. Etiquette

Cyber Security is the lock on your door. It’s the armor that keeps hackers, viruses, and "phishers" away from your private information.

Netiquette is how you treat people once you're inside the house. It's being kind, truthful, and helpful in the digital world.

Pro-Tip

"A good Kidventor is both safe and kind. You can't build the future if your tools are compromised!"

The Guardians' Protocol

🔑

Fortress Passwords

Don't use "123456" or your pet's name. Use Passphrases—a string of random words that are easy for you to remember but impossible for a robot to guess.

Example: BlueMonkey$Banana$Jump2026
🎣

Spot the Bait

If an email or message says you won a "Free iPhone" or asks for your password urgently, it's likely a Phishing Scam. Look at the sender's address carefully!

Rule: Think Before You Click.
📱

Two-Key Defense

Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA). This means even if someone steals your password, they still can't get in without the secret code sent to your phone.

Status: Highly Recommended
🤫

Digital Footprint

Everything you post stays forever. Never share your home address, school name, or current location with anyone you haven't met in the real world.

Rule: Keep it Private.

🌍 The Digital Village

"A shield is not just for yourself, but for your neighbors." When you follow safe practices, you prevent viruses and scams from spreading to your friends, family, and community. We are all guardians of the internet.
