What is Netiquette?

Learn the golden rules of how to behave in the digital world.

The Definition

Netiquette is a combination of the words Network and Etiquette. It is a set of rules for behaving properly online. Just as we use good manners at the dinner table or in the classroom, we must use good manners on the internet.

The 5 Golden Rules

🤝 Remember the Human

Never say anything online that you wouldn't say to someone's face. Behind every screen name is a real person with real feelings.

🤫 Respect Privacy

Do not share other people's secrets, photos, or private information without their permission. Keep your own passwords private too!

📢 Don't SHOUT

Writing in all capital letters (LIKE THIS) is considered shouting online. Use normal casing to be polite.

✨ Check Your Facts

Don't spread "Fake News." Always make sure information is true before you share it with your friends or family.

🌍 African Wisdom Online

"It takes a village to raise a child." In the digital village, we are all responsible for each other. When you are kind to someone in a comment or a chat, you are helping build a better digital world for everyone.
