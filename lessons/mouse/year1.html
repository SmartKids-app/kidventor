How to hold your mouse:

Rest your palm on the back of the mouse. Put your index finger on the left button. This is the finger you will use to pop the bubbles!

What is a "Click"?

A click is a quick press and release of the left mouse button. Try not to hold it down too long—just a quick "tap" with your finger!
