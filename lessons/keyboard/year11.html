The Navigator

Master the System Keys: TAB ENTER SPACE

GOAL: Tab to the first box

Step 1: The Jump

Press TAB to move your focus into the first box.

Step 2: Next Field

Press TAB again to move to "Job Title".

Step 3: The Toggle

Tab to the checkbox and press SPACE to check it.

Step 4: Execute

Tab to the button and press ENTER to finish.
