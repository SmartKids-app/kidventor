Home Row Mastery

What is the Home Row? It is the middle row of your keyboard. Your fingers should always "live" here so they can reach every other key quickly.

The Bumps: Feel your F and J keys. Do you feel the tiny bumps? Those are for your index fingers so you can find "Home" without looking!
