Mouse Mastery 🖱️

Lesson Progress 1 / 8

Lesson 1 — Mouse Parts

A computer mouse has important parts that help you control the screen.

  • • Left Button: Select & Open
  • • Right Button: Hidden Menus
  • • Scroll Wheel: Move Up/Down
  • • Sensor: Movement Detection

Lesson 2 — Single Click

Press the Left Button once. Use this to pick a toy or a game on your screen!

Lesson 3 — Double Click

Click-click! Press twice very quickly to open up a folder or start a new program.

Lesson 4 — Right Click

The Right Button is for Extra Options. It opens a secret list of things you can do with a file.

Lesson 5 — Drag and Drop

Hold the button down, move your mouse, then let go! It's like picking up a block and moving it to a new pile.
