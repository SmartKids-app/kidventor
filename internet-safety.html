Become an Internet Guard

The internet is powerful, but not everything online is safe. Learn how to spot fake websites, dangerous links, and protect your computer like a cyber hero!

🔒 https://www.kidventor.com.ng
Rule #1

Look for the “S” 🛡️

Before typing your password, always check the website address. HTTPS means the site is secure.

If you only see HTTP, or your browser shows a warning, stop immediately.

✅

The padlock means your information is encrypted and safer from hackers.

Think Before You Click 🖱️

Hackers use something called Phishing. They pretend to be your school, your game, or even your friend to trick you.

The Bait 🎣

“Click here now and get 1,000,000 free coins!”

The Shield 🛡️

Hover over the link first. If it looks strange or suspicious, do NOT click it.

Keep Your Computer Healthy 🩺

🚫

Unknown Downloads

Never download files from websites you do not trust. Hidden viruses can destroy your computer.

🔄

Keep Updating

Updates fix weak spots in your computer. Think of them like medicine for your device.

🔌

Stranger USBs

Never plug random USB drives into your computer. They may carry dangerous malware.

📶

Public Wi-Fi Danger

Open Wi-Fi in airports, cafés, and public places is like speaking loudly in a crowded room.

Never log into your bank, school portal, or passwords using public Wi-Fi.
