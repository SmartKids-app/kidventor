Study Smarter, Not Harder

Your brain is the world's most powerful computer. To get the best results, you need to install the right habits!

Step 1: The "No-Distraction" Zone 📵

When you study, your brain enters "Deep Work" mode. But every time your phone pings or someone talks to you, your brain "restarts."

📱

Phone Away

🎧

Quiet Spot

🚀

Deep Focus Mode

THE RECALL TEST

"Don't just re-read the page over and over..."
"Close the book and try to explain it to a friend (or your teddy bear!)"

Use "Active Recall" 💡

Reading a page 10 times doesn't help you remember. **Testing yourself** is the only way to make the information stick forever.

The 25-5 Method 🍅

⏱️

25 Mins Work

Set a timer and work with 100% focus. No talking!

🥤

5 Mins Break

Stretch, drink water, or walk around. No screens!

🔄

Repeat

Do this 4 times, then take a longer 30-minute break.

Sleep is Your "Save" Button 💾

When you sleep, your brain moves everything you learned today from "Short-Term Memory" to "Long-Term Memory." If you don't sleep, you lose the info!

⭐ Goal: 9 Hours of Sleep
